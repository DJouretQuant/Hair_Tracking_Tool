Contribute to the first high-quality open science scalp imaging database.

About the Project

Hair Change Tracker is a participant-powered research initiative to collect standardized, high-resolution scalp images over time. Phase 1 focuses on building the world’s first open science scalp imaging dataset to advance computer vision tools for understanding hair change patterns. Once we reach 100 participants, contributors will gain access to a tool for visualizing their own hair change journey.

Consent Summary

Read Consent Details
  • Your photos will be stored securely in Backblaze B2 cloud storage.
  • Images will be anonymized and stripped of EXIF metadata.
  • The dataset will be public for non-commercial research in Phase 1.
  • Participation is voluntary; you can withdraw at any time.

What This Tool Will Do (Phase 1)

  • Help researchers study patterns of hair change over time.
  • Lay the foundation for future AI analysis tools.
  • Support open science by sharing the dataset (non-commercial use).
