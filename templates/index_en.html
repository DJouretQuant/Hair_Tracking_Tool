About the Project

What This Tool Will Do (Phase 1)

  • Help researchers study patterns of hair change over time.
  • Lay the foundation for future AI analysis tools.
  • Support open science by sharing the dataset (non-commercial use).

Consent Form

By participating, you agree to provide standardized scalp photographs for research and dataset creation. Your data will be stored securely and used to develop open science resources and potential AI analysis tools. Images may be included in public datasets, stripped of any personally identifying information.

You may withdraw your participation at any time by contacting the project team, at which point your data will be deleted from the dataset. This is **not a diagnostic tool**, and results are not medical advice.
